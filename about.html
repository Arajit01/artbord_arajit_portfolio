My Story

Designing with Passion & Purpose.

Hello! I'm Arajit Halder, a creative Graphic Designer based in Kolkata, India. With over 10+ years of experience, I help brands communicate their unique stories through compelling visuals.

My journey started with a simple curiosity for art, which quickly turned into a career in digital design. I specialize in Branding, Logo Design, and UI/UX. I believe that good design is not just about how it looks, but how it works and how it makes you feel.

When I'm not designing, I'm exploring new design trends, mentoring junior designers, or finding inspiration in the vibrant culture of my city.
