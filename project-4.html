Artbord Studio Rebranding

Client

Artbord Co.

Services

Branding, UI/UX, Logo

Year

2026

The Challenge

The client needed a fresh, modern identity that spoke to a younger audience while maintaining their professional integrity. The existing logo was outdated, and the website lacked user engagement features.

My goal was to create a visual language that felt energetic, tech-forward, and trustworthy. We focused on bold typography and a vibrant color palette.

The Solution

I developed a complete brand guideline, including a new logo, typography system, and color scheme. The web design focused on minimalism with high-impact imagery.
